Apples

Description

These are some questionable looking apples, I will admit.

While they look abnormal, they may be worth your while. The recipe is as follows.

Ingrediets

  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 4 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored, and sliced

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add sugar and cinnamon; stir until sugar dissolves.
  2. Add apples and cook until softened, 5 to 8 minutes.
  3. EAT!!!
